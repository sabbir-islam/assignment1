Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Creating new ideas and using technology to improve lives. Helping industries turn ideas into real solutions.

Sustainability

Protecting the environment with green practices and clean energy. Growing in a way that cares for the planet.

Infrastructure

Building better roads, cities, and connections for everyone. Making life easier with smart planning.

Empowerment

Helping people through education and equal opportunities. Giving everyone the chance to succeed.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"We dream of a Bangladesh where opportunity knows no boundaries, and every citizen has the power to shape their future. A Bangladesh where innovation fuels progress, sustainability protects our resources, and empowerment uplifts every individual. Together, we will build a nation where poverty is a thing of the past, where every child has access to quality education, and where technology connects us to endless possibilities. It is time for us to join hands and turn this dream into reality, creating a Bangladesh that inspires the world."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
